The ASCA Recordings February 2010

The expelled students and their attorney Fred Gray spoke for the first time publicly about their case and the events that led to it at the Association of the Student Conduct Administration (ASCA) 2010 conference on the occasion of the 50th Anniversary of the events that became Dixon vs. The Alabama State Board of Education.

ASCA 2010 Opening Session – February 3, 2010

The Opening Session of the Association of the Student Conduct Administration (ASCA) conference on the occasion of the 50th Anniversary of the events that became Dixon vs. The Alabama State Board of Education. This was the first time the expelled students and their attorney Fred Gray spoke publicly about their case and the events that led to it.
